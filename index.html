07 — Helpful answers

Before your
first visit.

A few quick answers about visiting our dental clinic on FC Road. If yours is not here, message us.

What treatments are available at the clinic?+

We provide routine check-ups, cleaning and polishing, tooth-coloured fillings, root canal care, crowns and bridges, children’s consultations, smile design, Invisalign clear aligners and tooth jewellery.

Do you provide Invisalign treatment in Pune?+

Yes. Dr. Shweta Valunj is an official Invisalign provider. Your consultation includes an assessment of your smile, treatment suitability and the next steps for a personalised aligner plan.

Does tooth jewellery require drilling?+

Tooth jewellery is usually bonded to the tooth surface without drilling. We first check that the selected tooth is healthy, then explain care and removal before you decide.

Where is the dental clinic located on FC Road?

Dr. Shweta's Dental Practice is at FF No. 107, Sanas Memories Building, Ferguson College Road, Pune 411004, above BookBar. You can open the exact location from the map section below.

How do I book a dental appointment?

You can book online through the appointment buttons, message the clinic on WhatsApp, or call +91 86058 64503. If you are unsure which treatment to select, choose a general dental consultation.

What should I expect at my first consultation?

We begin by discussing your concern and relevant history, followed by a dental examination. The dentist then explains the findings, suitable options and any next steps. Treatment is only planned after an individual assessment.

Are clear aligners suitable for everyone?

Not every alignment or bite concern is best treated with clear aligners. Suitability depends on your teeth, gums, bite and treatment goals, so a clinical assessment is necessary before a recommendation.

How long will my dental treatment take?

Timelines vary by treatment and clinical complexity. Some procedures may be completed in one visit, while aligner or restorative plans can take longer. You will receive a more accurate estimate after examination.
